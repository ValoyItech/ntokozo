Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming the most trusted cleaning service in the metropolitan area

The Beginning

Luxe Domestic Services was born from Ntokozo's personal experience as a busy professional who struggled to maintain the pristine home environment she desired. Recognizing that many families and businesses faced the same challenge, she decided to create a solution that would exceed expectations.

What started as a small residential cleaning service has evolved into a comprehensive domestic services company, offering everything from regular house cleaning to professional nanny services and bulk cleaning supplies.

Today, Luxe Domestic Services stands as a testament to the power of dedication, quality service, and genuine care for our clients' needs. We've built lasting relationships with families and businesses who trust us with their most important spaces.
